Logo

Creating the logo is simple. 1st place outer thin black circle and a thicker teal circle placed on top for the boundary. Next two curved lines near the bottom are put together to resemble a finish line ribbon. Then an orange sunset is put over the ribbon followed by a black silhouette of a shoe (use back bound remover for) placed in front of the sunset. Finally using the Outlander Island Texture font, put Aira Labs on top and founded 2025. The program used for this was Canva which was used due to its accessibility, access to multiple images within the website, magic features such as a background remover, and ability to see the layers.
